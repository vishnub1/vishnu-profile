01 · About

The Engineer
Behind the Uptime

I'm a Site Reliability Engineer based in Pune, India, focused on keeping large-scale e-commerce production systems healthy, observable, and resilient. My day-to-day involves monitoring AWS infrastructure, hunting down incidents before they escalate, and collaborating closely with DevOps and application teams.

Currently at FirstCry.com — one of India's largest e-commerce platforms — I've been part of maintaining 99.9% uptime for critical systems while actively expanding into Docker, CI/CD pipelines, and Kubernetes to grow into a full DevOps–SRE hybrid role.

I believe reliability isn't just about fixing things when they break — it's about building systems that are so observable and automated, they rarely break in the first place.
